## 1. Executive Summary

Gardners Ground Maintenance is a sole-trader gardening and ground maintenance business operating from Roche, Cornwall. The business provides residential and light commercial garden services across the Cornwall area, specialising in lawn care, hedge management, garden clearances, and power washing.

The business operates as a one-man operation, with a maximum capacity of **3 jobs per day** (Monday–Saturday), or **1 full-day job** for larger projects. Revenue is generated through one-off bookings, recurring subscription packages, and seasonal services.

**Year 1 Revenue Target:** £41,500 – £52,000
**Break-even Point:** Month 3
**Net Profit Target (Year 1):** £26,000 – £35,000

## 8. Seasonal Strategy

### 8.1 Peak Season (April–October)

- **Focus:** Lawn cutting (volume), hedge trimming, garden makeovers
- **Target:** 12–14 one-off bookings/week + subscription clients
- **Upsell:** Striped finishes, edging, lawn treatments
- **Goal:** Build subscription client base to 10+

### 8.2 Off-Season (November–March)

- **Focus:** Garden clearances, power washing, hedge reduction, leaf clearing
- **Target:** 4–6 bookings/week
- **Strategy:** Offer winter deals (10% off clearances), push power washing before spring
- **Maintenance:** Equipment servicing, van MOT, marketing for spring rush

## 11. Risk Assessment

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Bad weather** | High (Cornwall!) | Medium | Diversify into indoor-adjacent work (power washing, clearances). Budget 4 weeks lost. |
| **Equipment breakdown** | Medium | High | Maintain equipment fund (£500/yr). Have backup hand tools. |
| **Seasonal income dip** | High | Medium | Build subscription base for steady winter income. Push clearances/washing. |
| **Injury / illness** | Low | Critical | Public liability insurance. Income protection insurance (consider). No sick pay = biggest risk. |
| **Client no-shows** | Medium | Low | Booking confirmation system with email/Telegram. Consider deposit for large jobs. |
| **Competition** | Medium | Low | Differentiate on professionalism, online booking, transparency. |
| **Van failure** | Low | High | Maintain breakdown cover. Budget for replacement at 3–5 years. |
