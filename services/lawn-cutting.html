Lawn Cutting in Cornwall

Pristine, striped lawns — every visit, every season

Get a Free Quote

A beautifully cut lawn is the centrepiece of any Cornish garden. At Gardners Ground Maintenance, we deliver professional lawn mowing and edging that leaves your grass looking immaculate — whether you have a modest cottage garden in St Austell or a sprawling estate on the Roseland Peninsula. Using professional-grade equipment, we achieve that sought-after striped finish that transforms the look and feel of your outdoor space.

Cornwall's mild, maritime climate means grass grows for much of the year — often well into November and starting again in early March. That extended growing season demands regular, reliable mowing to keep lawns looking their best. Our scheduled visit plans mean you never have to worry about your lawn getting out of control, even during the lush spring and summer growth spurts that our Cornish weather encourages.

Every visit includes a thorough mow at the optimal height for the season, precision edging along paths, borders and driveways, and full collection of clippings so your lawn is left spotless. We also carry out a quick visual health check, keeping an eye out for early signs of moss, disease or waterlogging — common issues in Cornwall's damp climate — so problems can be addressed before they take hold.

From regular fortnightly cuts for busy families in Truro and Falmouth to one-off tidies before a garden party in Padstow, we tailor every job to your needs. No lawn is too small, and no estate is too large. We bring the right equipment for the job, adjusting cutting heights seasonally and ensuring clean, sharp blades for a healthy, professional result every time.
