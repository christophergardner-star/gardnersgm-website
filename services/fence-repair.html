Fence Repair in Cornwall

Professional fence repair and replacement — keeping your garden secure

From £65

Living in Cornwall means living with some of the most dramatic weather in the UK. Atlantic storms batter the county throughout autumn and winter, and garden fences are often the first casualty. Whether you've lost a single panel overnight or your entire fence line has come down, Gardners Ground Maintenance provides a fast, professional fence repair service to get your boundary secure again.

We handle all types of fence repair and replacement — from individual panel swaps and broken post repairs to full fence line rebuilds. We work with closeboard, lap panel, picket, and post-and-rail fencing, using quality timber sourced from local suppliers wherever possible. Posts can be set in concrete or timber as required, and we always ensure a solid, lasting finish that can stand up to the Cornish elements.

Beyond storm damage, fences deteriorate over time due to moisture, rot, and general wear. We offer timber treatment and staining to extend the life of your existing fence, as well as trellis installation and gate repair or hanging. If your garden gate is sagging, sticking, or has blown off its hinges, we'll have it sorted quickly and affordably.

All old panels, posts, and debris are removed and disposed of responsibly as part of the job. We pride ourselves on a quick turnaround — because a broken fence isn't just unsightly, it's a security issue for pets, children, and your property. Get in touch for a free quote and we'll have your fence looking solid again in no time.
