Power Washing in Cornwall

Restore your surfaces to like-new condition with professional pressure washing

From £50

Cornwall's damp maritime climate is beautiful, but it takes a real toll on outdoor surfaces. Driveways, patios, decking, garden paths, and walls quickly become coated in algae, lichen, moss, and stubborn green film that makes surfaces slippery, unsightly, and even hazardous. Our professional power washing service uses commercial-grade pressure washing equipment to strip away years of build-up and restore your surfaces to their original condition.

We work on all types of hard surfaces — concrete, block paving, natural stone, Indian sandstone, porcelain tiles, timber decking, rendered walls, and conservatory roofs. Our equipment features adjustable pressure settings so we can blast tough grime off concrete at full power while using a gentler touch on delicate timber decking or aged stonework. Every surface gets the right treatment to deliver maximum cleaning without causing damage.

For block paving, we offer a complete restoration service including pressure washing, re-sanding of joints, and optional sealant application to protect against future growth and staining. For patios and driveways, we can apply a professional-grade surface treatment after washing that inhibits algae and moss regrowth for months, keeping your surfaces looking cleaner for longer — especially important in Cornwall's perpetually damp conditions.

The results speak for themselves. Customers are consistently amazed at the dramatic transformation — surfaces that looked permanently stained come up looking brand new. We serve homes, businesses, holiday lets, and commercial properties right across Cornwall, from Falmouth and the Roseland Peninsula to Padstow, Wadebridge, and the Camel Valley.
