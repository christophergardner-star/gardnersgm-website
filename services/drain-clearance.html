Drain Clearance in Cornwall

Professional domestic drain unblocking — keeping your water flowing freely

From £45

Blocked drains are one of the most common — and most frustrating — problems for homeowners in Cornwall. The county's clay-heavy soil and high annual rainfall create the perfect conditions for drainage issues. Leaves, silt, moss, and root ingress can clog garden drains, soakaways, surface water drains, and gullies, causing flooding, waterlogging, and potential damage to your property. Gardners Ground Maintenance offers a professional domestic drain clearance service to diagnose and resolve these problems quickly.

We deal with all types of domestic outdoor drainage issues — from a simple blocked gully to a completely overwhelmed soakaway. Our team will assess the problem, identify the cause of the blockage, and clear it using the most effective method. For stubborn blockages, we have pressure jetting equipment to blast through compacted debris and root material, restoring full flow to your drainage system.

Cornwall's lush vegetation is beautiful, but tree and shrub roots are one of the biggest causes of drain blockages in the county. We specialise in removing root ingress from drainage pipes and can advise on preventative measures to stop it recurring. We also fit leaf and silt traps to catch debris before it enters your drain system, significantly reducing the frequency of future blockages.

Please note that our drain clearance service covers domestic properties only — we do not handle industrial or commercial drainage. For persistent or recurring drainage problems that suggest a deeper issue such as collapsed pipes, we can refer you to a trusted partner for a CCTV drain survey. Every job comes with honest advice on what's causing the problem and how to prevent it happening again.
