Lawn Treatment in Cornwall

Feed, protect and transform your lawn — season by season

From £35

A truly healthy lawn needs more than just regular mowing — it needs the right nutrition and protection at the right time. At Gardners Ground Maintenance, we provide professional lawn feed, weed control and disease treatment programmes tailored to Cornwall's unique soil and climate conditions. Whether you're dealing with a dandelion invasion in Redruth or a moss-covered lawn in Helston, our targeted treatments deliver visible results.

Cornwall's soil is often acidic and slate-based, creating specific challenges that off-the-shelf products simply don't address. High rainfall leaches nutrients quickly, moss thrives in our damp conditions, and weeds like clover, buttercups and daisies can spread aggressively in the mild climate. We carry out soil analysis to understand your lawn's specific needs, then apply the correct treatments at the correct rates — no guesswork, no wasted product, no damage to your grass.

Our seasonal programme covers your lawn year-round: a nitrogen-rich spring feed to kick-start growth after winter; targeted summer weed control using selective herbicides that eliminate dandelions, clover, daisies and buttercups without harming the grass; autumn moss treatment with iron sulphate to tackle Cornwall's worst moss problems; and a winter hardener to strengthen roots and build resilience against frost and waterlogging. We use both granular and liquid application methods depending on conditions and treatment type.

All products we use are professional-grade and safe for children and pets once dry — typically within 1–2 hours. We'll always advise you on any precautions after treatment. From one-off weed treatments for homeowners in Bodmin and Liskeard to full annual programmes for customers across the whole of Cornwall, we make it easy to achieve the lush, weed-free lawn you've always wanted.
