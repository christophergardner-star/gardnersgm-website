When a tree comes down in a storm, it can't wait until Monday morning. Fallen trees block driveways, crush fences, damage roofs, and bring down power lines. In Cornwall, where Atlantic weather systems regularly deliver gale-force winds and heavy rain, emergency tree situations are an all-too-common occurrence. Gardners Ground Maintenance provides a 24/7 emergency tree surgery service to get you safe and clear as quickly as possible.

Our team responds to emergency call-outs across Cornwall, dealing with fallen trees blocking roads and driveways, dangerous branches hanging over properties, and storm-damaged trees that pose an immediate risk. We carry out safe chainsaw work using full PPE, and all our operatives are experienced and fully insured. Whether it's sectional felling of a large tree resting on a building, crown reduction to remove dangerous weight, or limb removal to clear access, we have the skills and equipment to handle it.

We understand that emergencies don't keep office hours. Our after-hours service runs from 6:30pm to 7:30am, with a 50% emergency surcharge applied. During standard hours, we aim for same-day response whenever possible. Where fallen trees affect highways or power lines, we work alongside local authorities and utility companies to coordinate a safe resolution.

After the emergency is dealt with, we handle the full clean-up — all timber, branches, and debris are cut up, removed, and disposed of. If the remaining stump needs grinding or the area needs making good, we can arrange that too. Don't risk tackling a fallen tree yourself — call us and let us handle it safely and professionally.
