Gutter Cleaning in Cornwall

Professional gutter and downpipe clearance — protecting your home from water damage

From £45

Gutters are one of those things most homeowners don't think about until something goes wrong — and by then, the damage is often already done. In Cornwall, with its heavy tree cover and high rainfall, gutters can fill up surprisingly fast. Leaves, moss, twigs, and even bird nests block the flow of water, causing it to overflow and run down walls, saturate foundations, and rot fascia boards. Gardners Ground Maintenance provides a thorough, professional gutter cleaning service to keep your home protected.

Our gutter cleaning service covers the full system: gutters, downpipes, and outlets. We remove all accumulated debris by hand and flush the system through to check for clear flow. If downpipes are blocked — a common problem caused by compacted leaf mulch — we clear them using specialist rods and water pressure. Every job is completed safely using appropriate ladder or scaffold access, and we take all debris away with us.

For properties surrounded by trees or in exposed locations, we also offer gutter guard and mesh fitting to dramatically reduce the amount of debris that enters your gutters in the first place. This is especially popular with customers in wooded areas of mid-Cornwall and the Fowey Valley, where leaf fall is relentless from September through to December. A one-off investment in gutter guards can save years of repeated cleaning costs.

We recommend gutter cleaning at least twice a year — once in spring to clear winter debris and check for damage, and again in late autumn after the leaves have fallen. As part of every job, we provide a photo report showing the condition of your gutters before and after cleaning, giving you full visibility of the work completed. Blocked gutters cause damp, leaks, fascia rot, and even foundation damage — don't let a £45 job turn into a £5,000 repair bill.
