Garden Clearance in Cornwall

Reclaim your outdoor space — complete clearance from overgrown jungle to blank canvas

From £100

Whether you've just moved into a new property, inherited a neglected garden, or simply let things get out of hand over the years, our professional garden clearance service will transform your outdoor space back to a clean, usable area. Cornwall's mild, wet climate means gardens can become overgrown remarkably quickly — what was once a neat border can turn into an impenetrable thicket of brambles, ivy, and self-seeded trees in just a couple of seasons.

Our experienced team handles every type of garden clearance, from light tidying and border clearing through to heavy-duty work involving dense bramble, established ivy, tree stumps, and accumulated rubbish. We work with landlords managing rental properties, estate agents preparing homes for sale, tenants facing end-of-tenancy obligations, and homeowners ready to reclaim their gardens for family use. No job is too big or too overgrown for our crew.

All waste is removed responsibly and in full compliance with environmental regulations. Green waste is composted or sent to licensed recycling centres, and we can arrange skip hire for larger jobs involving mixed or non-organic waste. We leave your garden clear, level, and ready for whatever comes next — whether that's laying a new lawn, planting borders, or simply enjoying an open, tidy space.

Every clearance job includes before-and-after photographs so you can see the full extent of the transformation. For larger or more complex sites, we'll carry out an initial survey and provide a detailed quote with no hidden extras. Our team covers all areas of Cornwall, from Penzance and St Ives to Bodmin, Truro, Newquay, and beyond.
