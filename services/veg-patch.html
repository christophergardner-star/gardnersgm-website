Vegetable Patch Preparation in Cornwall

We prepare the perfect growing space — you enjoy the harvest

From £70

There's nothing quite like stepping into your own garden and picking fresh vegetables for dinner. Cornwall's exceptionally mild climate, warmed by the Gulf Stream, makes it one of the best regions in the UK for growing your own food — with a longer growing season than almost anywhere else in the country. Whether you're a complete beginner dreaming of your first tomato plant or an experienced grower ready for a full kitchen garden, we'll prepare the perfect growing space for you.

Our vegetable patch preparation service covers everything from initial ground clearance and weed removal through to soil conditioning, levelling, and construction of raised beds. We rotavate compacted ground, work in quality compost and organic matter to improve soil structure, and install weed membrane where needed to give your plants the best possible start. For raised beds, we build using pressure-treated timber, railway sleepers, or brick — whatever suits your garden style and budget.

We handle all the heavy lifting so you can focus on the enjoyable part — choosing what to grow and watching it flourish. Our team can advise on bed orientation for maximum sunlight, drainage solutions for heavy Cornish clay soils, and companion planting strategies that naturally deter pests. We also offer polytunnel base preparation for those looking to extend their growing season even further with undercover cultivation.

This service is ideal for families wanting to teach children where food comes from, retirees looking for a rewarding hobby, eco-conscious households aiming to reduce food miles, and anyone who simply loves the taste of home-grown produce. We prepare it — you plant it, tend it, and enjoy the incredible satisfaction of eating food you grew yourself.
