Weeding Treatment in Cornwall

Take back control of your borders, beds, and paths with professional weed management

From £40

Weeds are the single biggest headache for Cornish gardeners. Our warm, wet maritime climate creates the perfect conditions for aggressive weed growth — and once they take hold, they spread fast. Dandelions, bindweed, ground elder, couch grass, nettles, docks, and invasive species like Japanese knotweed can quickly overwhelm even the most lovingly tended garden. Our professional weeding treatment service tackles every type of weed problem, restoring order to your outdoor spaces.

We use a combination of proven techniques tailored to your specific situation. For flower borders and vegetable beds, we carry out careful hand weeding to remove weeds without disturbing your prized plants. For paths, driveways, and gravel areas, we apply professional-grade selective herbicides that target weeds at the root while leaving surrounding plants unharmed. For persistent perennial weeds with deep root systems, we use targeted spot treatments that kill the entire root structure, not just the visible growth.

Prevention is just as important as removal. After clearing your weeds, we can apply a thick layer of quality bark mulch or decorative gravel over weed membrane to suppress regrowth for months. Mulching not only keeps weeds at bay but also retains soil moisture, regulates temperature, and gives borders a clean, professional finish. We supply and spread mulch as part of the service — you don't need to source anything yourself.

We also offer eco-friendly and child/pet-safe treatment options for families concerned about chemical use. For customers who want ongoing weed management, we provide regular maintenance programmes — monthly or seasonal visits to keep weeds consistently under control before they become a problem. If we identify Japanese knotweed on your property, we'll provide a specialist referral for safe, legally compliant treatment and removal.
