Hedge Trimming in Cornwall

Neat, shapely hedges that define your garden beautifully

Get a Free Quote

Well-maintained hedges are a hallmark of Cornwall's beautiful gardens and countryside lanes. At Gardners Ground Maintenance, we provide expert hedge cutting, shaping and reduction for all hedge types — from neatly sculpted box hedging in Fowey to towering leylandii boundaries in Newquay. Whether your hedge needs a light trim or a major reduction, our experienced team delivers clean, precise results every time.

Cornwall's mild, wet climate creates ideal growing conditions for hedges, which means they can quickly become overgrown and unruly if left unchecked. Privet, laurel, beech, hawthorn and leylandii all thrive in our Cornish conditions, often putting on several feet of growth each year. Regular trimming not only keeps your garden looking sharp but also promotes denser, healthier growth and prevents hedges from encroaching on paths, driveways and neighbouring properties.

We handle every aspect of the job: shaping, topping, side trimming and, where needed, hard reduction to bring overgrown hedges back under control. Our team uses professional petrol and battery-powered hedge trimmers for clean cuts that heal quickly and encourage bushier regrowth. For tall hedges, we carry proper access equipment and follow strict safety protocols — no ladders balanced on uneven ground.

Every job is left spotless. All clippings are collected, the ground is swept or raked clean, and green waste is removed from site. From regular maintenance schedules for homeowners across Truro, Penzance and Bodmin to one-off reductions for landlords and estate agents, we've got your hedges covered.
