About Gardners Ground Maintenance

Cornwall's trusted gardener — real people, real results, real passion for the outdoors

A Proper Cornwall Gardener

Gardners Ground Maintenance started with a simple idea: honest, reliable gardening done to the highest standard — at a fair price. Based in Roche, PL26, right in the heart of Cornwall, we're building a reputation that speaks for itself.

We're not a franchise and we're not a faceless company. We're a local team that turns up on time, does a proper job, and genuinely cares about how your garden looks when we leave. Whether it's regular lawn care, a one-off garden clearance, or a specialist treatment to bring a tired lawn back to life — we treat every garden like it's our own.

We serve homeowners, landlords, holiday lets, commercial properties and parish councils across every corner of Cornwall. No job too small, no garden too far.
