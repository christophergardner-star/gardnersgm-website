About Us

Passionate about gardens, dedicated to quality — serving Cornwall with pride

Your Local Gardening Experts

Gardners Ground Maintenance is a professional gardening and grounds maintenance company proudly serving customers across Cornwall. With over a decade of experience, we have built a reputation for reliability, quality workmanship, and a genuine passion for transforming outdoor spaces.

Whether you need regular lawn maintenance, a one-off garden clearance, specialist treatment to bring your lawn back to life, vegetable patch preparation, fence repairs, drain clearance, gutter cleaning or even emergency tree surgery — our experienced team has the skills and equipment to deliver outstanding results every time. For bigger jobs needing road traffic management, we can arrange streetworks applications and TM plans too.

We're not just about cutting grass. We offer a full range of ground maintenance services designed for homeowners, landlords, holiday lets, commercial properties and parish councils across Cornwall. From weekly lawn subscriptions to one-off emergency call-outs, we're here when you need us — including after-hours emergency availability from 6:30pm to 7:30am.

What We Do

A comprehensive range of gardening and grounds maintenance services

Lawn Care

Cutting, edging, scarifying, treatments, overseeding. Everything your lawn needs to look lush and green all year.

Hedges & Borders

Hedge trimming, shaping, height reduction and border weeding. Keeping boundaries tidy and gardens well-defined.

Vegetable Patches

From clearing ground to building raised beds and enriching soil — we'll prepare the perfect growing space for your veg.

Fence Repairs

Panel replacement, post repair, staining and gravel boards. We fix storm damage and age-related wear quickly and neatly.

Power Washing

Patios, driveways, decking and paths blasted clean. Sealant and re-sanding available to protect surfaces long-term.

Emergency Tree Surgery

Fallen trees, dangerous branches — we respond quickly, including after-hours call-outs from 6:30pm to 7:30am. A 50% emergency surcharge applies outside normal hours.

Drain Clearance

Blocked or slow garden drains? We clear single drains, handle root ingress and offer pressure jetting. Domestic drains only — not industrial.

Gutter Cleaning

Blocked gutters cause damp and damage. We clear leaves, moss and debris from gutters and downpipes, with optional gutter guard fitting to keep them clear longer.
